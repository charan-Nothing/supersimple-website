often with some level of intelligence.

A humanoid robot is a type of robot designed to resemble a human in form and function.
These robots typically have two arms, two legs, a torso, and a head, and are designed to move and interact in ways similar to humans.
Humanoid robots may be used in a variety of applications, including manufacturing, research, and entertainment.
